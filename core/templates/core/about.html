About GT Job Finder

GT Job Finder helps students and early-career professionals discover roles, track applications, and showcase skills — while enabling recruiters to post jobs and connect with qualified candidates.

What you can do

  • Create a profile with skills, education, and experience
  • Search jobs with filters (remote, visa sponsorship, salary)
  • Apply with one click and add a tailored note
  • Track status: Applied → Review → Interview → Offer → Closed
  • Control profile privacy

Why we built it

We want a simple, student-friendly job platform that surfaces relevant roles quickly and keeps the application process organized — without extra noise.
